About PolyHack

PolyHack is a 24‑hour, non‑code hackathon that unites students from Materials Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Biotechnology & Food Engineering, and Architecture to solve real polymer‑industry challenges. Teams turn scientific insight into slide‑deck concepts—no lab work, no software builds—strengthening the Technion ↔ Industry pipeline.

Who Can Participate?

Open to all Technion students in the four faculties above. Multidisciplinary teams of 4–5 students will be formed to maximise diversity and innovation.

Industry Challenges

Smart Thermochromic Packaging

Owner: Kafrit
Goal: ΔE < 3 after 5 cycles (80↔5 °C)

Long‑Acting Drug Release

Owners: Polyram & BioGen
Goal: t½ ≈ 30 days @ 37 °C

Biodegradable Thermoplastics

Owner: Carmel Olefins
Goal: ASTM D6400 ≤ 180 days

Lightweight TIM Composite

Owners: Tower & NVIDIA
Goal: κ > 8 W/m·K; ρ < 1.3 g/cc

Lab Waste Upcycling

Owner: Stratasys
Goal: ≥ 70 % material recovery

Judging & Prizes

Teams are evaluated on scientific innovation, technical feasibility, business/environmental impact, multidisciplinary teamwork, KPI compliance, and storytelling. Attractive prizes and industry recognition await the winning teams.
